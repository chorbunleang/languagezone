Why it works

Writing by hand is how characters stick

Forming each stroke builds muscle memory and trains your eye to recognise the shape — far more effective than reading alone.

  • 1 Trace faded characters to learn the correct shape and order.
  • 2 Write from memory in the empty squares to lock it in.
  • 3 Say the pinyin aloud to link sound, meaning, and movement.
