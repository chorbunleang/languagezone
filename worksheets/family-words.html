Chinese Family Words Worksheet

Family vocabulary is one of the first things every Chinese learner needs. Practise the essential words below — many are friendly repeated syllables that are easy to remember — then print your tracing sheet.

Notice how many family words repeat the same character twice — 爸爸, 妈妈, 哥哥. This makes them quick to learn and great early writing practice, since you write each character twice naturally.
