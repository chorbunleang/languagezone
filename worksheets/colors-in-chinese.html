Colors in Chinese

Colors are one of the most useful and fun things to learn early. Practise writing the essential Chinese color characters below — add 色 (sè, “color”) after most of them to name the color, as in 红色 (red).

色sècolor (红色 = red)
In everyday Chinese, most colors are said with 色 (sè) after them — 红色 (hóngsè) = red, 蓝色 (lánsè) = blue. A few, like 黑 and 白, are often used on their own.
