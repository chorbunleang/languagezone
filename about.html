About LanguageZone

LanguageZone builds free, carefully-made tools that help people learn to read and write Chinese.

Our mission

Learning to write Chinese characters should be simple and accessible to everyone. We create clean, practical tools — starting with our Chinese Worksheet Maker — that let learners, teachers, and parents generate exactly the practice material they need, in seconds, for free. No sign-up, no clutter, just useful tools that work.

Who we are

LanguageZone is run by a small team with a background in language education. We spend our days helping students learn, and we build these tools to solve the same problems we see in real classrooms: the need for good handwriting practice, clear stroke order, and materials that meet learners where they are. Every tool on this site is something we would want to use ourselves.

What we're building

We started with printable handwriting worksheets, and we are steadily expanding into a wider set of Chinese learning tools — from pinyin and stroke-order helpers to reading practice. Our goal is to become a genuinely useful, trustworthy home for anyone learning the language.

Get in touch

We welcome feedback, corrections, and suggestions — they genuinely make the tools better. You can reach us any time through our contact page.
