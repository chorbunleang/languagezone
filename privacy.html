Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LanguageZone.co ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you visit our website. Our tools work in your browser, and you do not need to create an account or give us personal details to use them.

Information we collect

We do not ask you to register or submit personal information to use our tools. The characters you type into the Worksheet Maker are processed in your own browser and are not sent to or stored on our servers.

Like most websites, our hosting and analytics providers may automatically collect standard technical information such as your browser type, device type, general location (country or region), and the pages you visit. This is used only to understand how the site is used and to keep it running well.

Cookies

A cookie is a small file placed on your device. We and our partners may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure traffic, and — where advertising is shown — to display relevant ads. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Advertising and Google AdSense

We may display advertising on this site, including through Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' cookies at www.aboutads.info.

Analytics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics tools to see how many people visit our pages and which tools are most useful. This information is aggregated and is not used to personally identify you.

Children's privacy

Our site is intended for a general audience and is often used by teachers and parents. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

Your choices

You can browse and use our tools without providing personal information. You can manage cookies through your browser, and opt out of personalised advertising using the links above.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@languagezone.co.
